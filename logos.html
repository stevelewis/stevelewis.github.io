Logos

Logos are so fun. They involve storytelling, typography, minimalism and marketing all at once. An entire mission statement has to be distilled into a mark. Here are a couple logos I made for some budding new businesses.

Wild Science Bread Co.

Wild Science Bread Company is the home business of my good friend, Nick Malburg. He started out selling bread subscriptions and advertising through instagram and quickly decided he wanted an identifiable brand to help him stick.
