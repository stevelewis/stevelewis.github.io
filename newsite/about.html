About

Since 2014, I've been doing design work of every kind at Chartio, a data visualization tool for companies. Before this, I worked with a startup, Designly, which began by selling Bootstrap themes and evolved into an online website builder.

I originally studied graphic design in college, and like most designers, I still like to get my hands dirty now and again. I've also collaborated with musicians to create album covers, like Nick Malburg and Half Man. Speaking of music, I was inspired by this site and designed covers for my favorite album of 2016, and am currently working on 2017.
