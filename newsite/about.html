About Me

Lately, I've been doing design work of every sort at Chartio, a data visualization tool for companies. Before that I worked with a startup called Designly, which started out making Bootstrap themes and evolved into a WYSIWYG website builder.

I originally studied Graphic Design in college, and still love working with print too. Over the years I've been lucky enough to work with some really cool groups and make designs for them (like in this project ). I've also collaborated with musicians to create album covers, like Nick Malburg and Half Man. I also was inspired by this site and designed covers for my favorite album of 2016, and am currently working on this year's.
