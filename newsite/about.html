About

Since 2014, I've been doing design work of every kind Chartio, a data visualization tool for companies. Before this, I worked with a startup called Designly, which began by selling Bootstrap themes and evolved into an online website builder.

Before all that, I lived in Pennsylvania, making logos and working at sign shops – my first real job out of college was at a pennysaver. I studied graphic design and screenprinting in college and made a comic book once.
